The L.A.D.

(Look Alike Droid)

The L.A.D is a humanoid robot that copies your arm movements. A true follower spirit.

Created by Mika Gazit, Mia Chevere, Elisa Camacho, Danny Kennen, and Zara Coakley

Project Objectives:

- Create a humanoid robot with a full range of arm movement

- Create a robot that is able to copy the arm movements of a person in front of it
